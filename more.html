My Hobbies & Interests

I’m passionate about capturing the beauty of food and scenery through photography and videography, turning everyday moments into visual stories, because, as they say, "Every picture tells a story." I love creating moments that speak without words.

Capturing food and scenery through my lens is more than a hobby — it’s my way of freezing time and celebrating life’s wonders. After all, "Photography is the art of making memories tangible."
